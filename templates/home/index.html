    Know your options: Emergency Room vs. Urgent Care

    When to choose Urgent Care
    • Choosing an urgent care is a convenient option when you need to see a doctor after normal hours or during weekends or even when you don’t have quick access to your regular physician. Most of urgent care facilities offer some onsite diagnostic tests which can provide you with a faster service at a better price. Please note that urgent cares are not suitable options in case of life-threatening conditions.
    When to go to Emergency Room (ER)
    • Emergency rooms are the best choice for treating severe medical conditions such as stroke, heart attack, severe bleeding, accidental injuries, poisoning, vomiting blood and any other severe medical situations. Being open 247, ERs offer the wide range of emergency services/tests with many specialists on call. Usually ERs are crowded and since they offer specialized care you may wait longer and end up paying more to get treated.
